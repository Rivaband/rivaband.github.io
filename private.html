Aquarama is a combination of modern funk-rnb grooves with vintage-taste melodies and arrangements typical of 60s surf-pop.


Born one year ago, Milan-based duo literally burnt the stages jumping directly from local to national-wide exposure: Aquarama is always keeping an eye on the world thanks to the innate international attitude of their music that led the duo achieve a prestigious collaboration with the New York disco reference dj In Flagranti and play beside french iconic band La Femme and canadian nu-jazz ambassadors BadBadNotGood.


The debut album “Riva”, released on April 21st and mastered by Grammy awarded Giovanni Versari, is now played on a 30-gigs italian tour and is ready to trace the route over Europe next autumn/winter.
