Aquarama is a combination of black-tropical grooves and 60s nuanced melodies: it's like a urban day-dream of a beach-party where fresh balearic melodies lie upon downtempo rhythms made in USA.


Born three years ago, Milan-based duo literally burnt the stages jumping directly from local to national-wide exposure: Aquarama is always keeping an eye on the world thanks to the innate international attitude of their music that led the duo achieve a prestigious collaboration with the New York disco reference dj In Flagranti and play beside french iconic band La Femme and canadian nu-jazz ambassadors BadBadNotGood.


The debut album “Riva”, released on April 21st and mastered by Grammy awarded Giovanni Versari, is played on a 80-gigs tour around Europe thanks to the collaboration with french label Sand Music.
